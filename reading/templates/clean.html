Day 159: Clean by Dr. Alejandro Junger

Introduction

In today’s modern world, we’re surrounded by toxins—from the air we breathe to the food we eat. In Clean, integrative medicine pioneer Dr. Alejandro Junger outlines a powerful detoxification program designed to restore your body’s natural ability to heal. Drawing from both functional medicine and ancient healing traditions, Junger offers a 21-day plan to reset your gut, boost energy, and clear your mind.

Published in 2009, Clean has become a cult classic among wellness seekers. It argues that many modern illnesses—fatigue, allergies, skin conditions, digestive disorders, and brain fog—are not diseases but symptoms of toxic overload and poor digestion.

Summary

  • Modern life overwhelms detox systems: Environmental toxins, stress, processed foods, and medication strain the liver and gut.
  • The Clean Program: A 21-day detox using an elimination diet, liquid meals, solid lunch, hydration, and gentle supplements.
  • Elimination Diet: Removes common irritants like gluten, dairy, sugar, alcohol, caffeine, soy, and processed foods.
  • Liquid meals: Smoothies or soups for breakfast and dinner ease digestion and support detox.
  • 12-hour digestive rest: A 12-hour fast between dinner and the next day’s breakfast promotes repair.

Core Concept

The core message of Clean is: When we reduce our toxic load and give the body a break from constant digestion, it naturally heals, restores balance, and thrives.

This approach prioritizes cleansing over calorie counting, supporting the body’s natural detoxification pathways and self-healing processes.

Key Insights and Strategies

  • Digestion is energy-intensive: Liquid meals reduce digestive burden, freeing energy for detox and repair.
  • Inflammation often starts in the gut: Gut permeability and food sensitivities drive chronic health issues.
  • Symptoms are signals: Skin rashes, fatigue, or anxiety may stem from toxic overload and poor digestion.
  • Elimination diet is diagnostic: Removing and reintroducing foods reveals personal triggers.
  • Mind-body support matters: Stress, poor sleep, and negative thoughts hinder the detox process.

Quotes to Remember

“The body knows how to heal. We just need to get out of the way.”
“Illness is not just bad luck or bad genes—it’s often the result of too much burden and not enough support.”
“What you take out of your diet is just as important as what you put in.”

Practical Application

  • Try a 21-day clean reset: Replace breakfast and dinner with smoothies or soups, and have a whole-food lunch.
  • Remove common irritants: Cut out sugar, gluten, dairy, caffeine, soy, and alcohol temporarily.
  • Hydrate generously: Drink at least 2 liters of water daily to support detox and digestion.
  • Support elimination: Take probiotics, enzymes, fiber, and practice deep breathing and light movement.
  • Be mindful: Stress reduction and sleep are as vital as clean eating during the detox process.

Interesting Facts and Stories

  • Inspired by his own health crisis: Junger developed Clean after suffering from IBS, allergies, and depression in medical residency.
  • Skin is a detox organ: Acne, rashes, or eczema can reflect internal toxicity trying to exit the body.
  • Detox symptoms are normal: Temporary fatigue or headaches in the first days can signal progress.
  • Celebrity following: Gwyneth Paltrow and Donna Karan have credited the Clean Program for renewed health.
  • The gut-liver axis is central: These two organs manage digestion, detox, hormones, and immunity.
