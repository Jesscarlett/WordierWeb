Day 161: 10-Day Green Smoothie Cleanse by JJ Smith

Introduction

The 10-Day Green Smoothie Cleanse by certified nutritionist and weight loss expert JJ Smith is a bestselling detox program that promises quick results: up to 15 pounds lost in 10 days, improved energy, reduced cravings, and clearer skin. Published in 2014, the book outlines a structured 10-day plan of nutrient-packed smoothies, light snacks, and clean living practices designed to reset the body, jumpstart weight loss, and break sugar addiction.

Smith presents the cleanse not just as a weight-loss tool, but as a means to heal the body, repair digestion, and build a healthier relationship with food.

Summary

  • Daily routine: 3 green smoothies per day (breakfast, lunch, dinner), water (64 oz), detox tea, and approved snacks.
  • Two versions: Full Cleanse (smoothies only) and Modified Cleanse (one healthy meal per day).
  • Supportive tips: Includes shopping lists, detox symptom management, and journaling tools.
  • Focus areas: Curbing sugar cravings, reducing inflammation, and boosting metabolism.

Core Concept

The core message of 10-Day Green Smoothie Cleanse is: Flooding your body with raw, plant-based nutrients gives your digestive system a break and helps eliminate toxins, reduce cravings, and burn fat.

Removing processed food while boosting hydration and fiber allows your body to reset and heal naturally.

Key Insights and Strategies

  • Green smoothies = fast nutrition: Blended raw greens and fruits deliver enzymes, fiber, and vitamins efficiently.
  • Cravings are chemically driven: Sugar and flour alter brain chemistry and hunger hormones.
  • Water is essential: Proper hydration flushes toxins and prevents fatigue or constipation.
  • Detox symptoms are part of the process: Headaches or low energy often resolve by Day 3.
  • Accountability boosts success: JJ recommends online groups, journaling, and buddy systems.

Quotes to Remember

“Your body has the power to heal itself. You just have to give it the right tools.”
“You’re not addicted to food—you’re addicted to the chemicals in food.”
“If you stay ready, you don’t have to get ready.”

Practical Application

  • Start with the Full or Modified Cleanse: Choose the level of intensity that suits your lifestyle and health needs.
  • Prep smoothies in advance: Use JJ’s recipes with leafy greens, fruit, flaxseed, and stevia (optional).
  • Stick to approved snacks: Raw veggies, boiled eggs, or unsalted nuts only—no processed foods.
  • Hydrate and sip detox tea: Drink at least 64 oz of water and a cup of herbal detox tea daily.
  • Journal your journey: Use the cleanse log to track energy, hunger, mood, and motivation.

Interesting Facts and Stories

  • JJ Smith's transformation: After health struggles, JJ lost over 60 pounds using green smoothies and natural detox methods.
  • Viral success: The cleanse gained popularity through social media and JJ’s online community, helping thousands achieve weight loss.
  • Detox side effects are expected: Temporary symptoms like fatigue and irritability are signs of sugar withdrawal.
  • Smoothie recipes are flexible: Add herbs, protein, or spices to personalize flavor and function.
  • Faith and mindset matter: JJ emphasizes daily affirmations and visualization to stay positive and focused.
