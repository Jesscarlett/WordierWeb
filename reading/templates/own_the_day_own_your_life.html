Day 173: Own the Day, Own Your Life by Aubrey Marcus

Introduction

Own the Day, Own Your Life by Aubrey Marcus, founder of Onnit, is a 24-hour playbook for optimizing your energy, focus, fitness, nutrition, and emotional wellbeing. Published in 2018, the book guides readers through a day of intentional living, with the idea that mastering just one day can lead to a lifetime of success and vitality. The tone is motivational, science-informed, and filled with actionable routines.

Summary

  • Wake up with sunlight and hydration: Reset your circadian rhythm and rehydrate with sea salt and lemon water.
  • Move early: Gentle morning movement increases circulation and mental alertness.
  • Eat smart breakfast: Ditch sugary carbs for fats and protein to stabilize energy and focus.
  • Work in flow: Protect your productivity window with focused, single-tasking strategies.
  • Eat mindfully through the day: Choose whole, nutrient-dense foods and consider carb timing or intermittent fasting.
  • Take active breaks: Walk, stretch, or breathe deeply between tasks to recharge.
  • Connect and unplug: Prioritize real-world relationships over digital distractions.
  • Wind down for sleep: Create a sleep ritual with dim light, calm activities, and screen-free time.

Core Concept

The core message of Own the Day, Own Your Life is: You don’t need to change your entire life overnight—just win the next 24 hours. With intentional habits, every day becomes an opportunity to optimize health, performance, and joy.

Key Insights and Strategies

  • Start strong: Morning sunlight and hydration set the tone for energy and focus.
  • Food is fuel: Choose meals that support metabolic health and cognition, not convenience.
  • Movement builds momentum: Micro-workouts beat sedentary lifestyles for energy and longevity.
  • Attention is precious: Minimize digital noise and reclaim your focus.
  • Sleep fuels all: Quality sleep is the base of every performance and wellness goal.

Quotes to Remember

“You are what you repeatedly do. Own your day, and you own your life.”
“Master your morning, and the rest of the day falls into place.”
“You don’t rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems.”

Practical Application

  • Hydrate on waking: Drink 12–16 oz. of water with lemon and sea salt first thing.
  • Do light movement: Get outside, stretch, and breathe to energize your body and mind.
  • Eat a clean breakfast: Include eggs, avocado, or nuts—skip the cereal and toast.
  • Batch and focus: Use time blocks and avoid notifications during deep work.
  • Sleep like a pro: Set a cutoff time for screens, use soft lighting, and wind down mindfully.

Interesting Facts and Stories

  • Aubrey’s transformation: He overcame burnout and self-destructive cycles through daily optimization.
  • Saltwater hydration: Adding minerals to morning water helps adrenal and brain function.
  • “Microdosing” movement: Short, frequent activity beats long, infrequent gym sessions.
  • Connection matters: Hugs and social time lower cortisol and boost wellbeing.
  • Sleep = high performance: Top performers make sleep a non-negotiable priority.
