Introduction

The Magic of Motherhood by Ashlee Gadd is a heartfelt collection of essays exploring the emotional complexity of motherhood. Blending honesty, tenderness, and poetic storytelling, Gadd portrays motherhood as both magical and messy—a journey filled with love, sacrifice, identity shifts, and transformative moments.
