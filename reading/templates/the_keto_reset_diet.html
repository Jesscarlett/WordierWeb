Day 165: The Keto Reset Diet by Mark Sisson

Introduction

The Keto Reset Diet by health pioneer Mark Sisson is a comprehensive guide to using the ketogenic diet to reset your metabolism, burn fat efficiently, and boost energy and mental clarity. Published in 2017, the book offers a “slow and strategic” approach that begins with a low-carb, primal foundation before transitioning into full ketosis.

Combining evolutionary science with practical strategies, Sisson promotes metabolic flexibility—the ability to efficiently burn both carbs and fat—as the key to long-term health and vitality.

Summary

  • Phase 1: Primal Preparation – A 21-day clean eating plan (low-carb, no sugar, no grains) to stabilize blood sugar and begin fat adaptation.
  • Phase 2: Keto Reset – Transition to <50g carbs/day, moderate protein, and healthy fats to enter nutritional ketosis.
  • Phase 3: Keto for Life – Maintain or cycle in and out of keto depending on your goals, activity level, and preferences.
  • Additional guidance: Includes meal plans, recipes, workout tips, sleep advice, and stress management techniques.

Core Concept

The core message of The Keto Reset Diet is: You don’t need to be in ketosis forever, but resetting your metabolism with keto improves your body’s ability to burn fat, manage hunger, and optimize energy.

This is not about deprivation—it’s about regaining metabolic freedom and control.

Key Insights and Strategies

  • Metabolic flexibility is the goal: Train your body to switch between carbs and fat for fuel seamlessly.
  • Ease into ketosis: Start with a low-carb, primal diet to avoid “keto flu.”
  • Keto is not a fat binge: Focus on quality fats, moderate protein, and nutrient-dense veggies.
  • Lifestyle affects results: Sleep, stress, and movement all impact metabolic health and keto adaptation.
  • Electrolytes are essential: Supplement with sodium, magnesium, and potassium to support energy and hydration.

Quotes to Remember

“Ketosis is your natural, ancestral metabolic state—it's not a trend, it's your birthright.”
“Keto isn’t about eating more fat. It’s about becoming better at burning it.”
“The goal isn’t just fat loss—it’s metabolic freedom.”

Practical Application

  • Start with a 21-day primal phase: Cut out sugar, grains, and processed foods. Increase vegetables, protein, and healthy fats.
  • Lower carbs gradually: Reduce intake to <50g/day only once you’re fat-adapted.
  • Eat whole, clean foods: Grass-fed meats, wild fish, eggs, avocados, olive oil, nuts, and leafy greens.
  • Stay hydrated and mineralized: Use bone broth, sea salt, and magnesium to avoid keto flu.
  • Exercise smart: Walk daily, lift heavy things, and sprint occasionally.

Interesting Facts and Stories

  • Sisson’s journey: As a former elite endurance athlete, he burned out on high-carb eating and regained his health through primal and keto living.
  • Ketones fuel brain performance: Many users report sharper mental focus, less anxiety, and more stable mood on keto.
  • Flexible keto: You don’t have to stay in ketosis forever—Sisson encourages seasonal or cyclical approaches.
  • Keto supports effortless fat loss: Ketones suppress hunger and boost fat oxidation without calorie counting.
  • The book launched a gentler keto movement: Unlike crash keto plans, Sisson promotes personalization and preparation.
